Wedding events

The engagement ceremony

  • Yen Dong, Y Yen, Nam Dinh
  • Mar 17 2023

Vietnamese Engagement Party, which is when the parents announce the couple are getting married. It starts off with the groom’s family preparing and bringing gifts to the bride’s family. Once there, they will ask for permission for their son to marry the daughter

The first party

  • Yen Dong, Y Yen, Nam Dinh
  • Mar 18 2023

The party is organized before the wedding. Groom and bride families will invite their friends, neighborhoods,... to home and enjoy the party.

The wedding party

  • Yen Dong, Y Yen, Nam Dinh
  • Mar 19 2023

On the wedding day, the groom’s family and some of their close friends will come and receive the bride at her house. After a formal tea and candle ceremony and some speeches, the couple would turn to their parents, receiving their advice and bowing to them. The groom’s mother would give the bride jewelry for good fortune.
